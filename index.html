 ║    THE MAY 1ST ASCII MUGUET TRADITION    ║
 ╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝

In France, May 1st is a special day called "La Fête du Muguet" (The Lily of the Valley Festival). Since 1561, people have been exchanging these delicate flowers for good luck!

On this day, street vendors set up little stands all over Paris and other French towns, selling fresh bunches of muguet. It's one of the only days anyone can sell flowers without a license!

The tradition is simple: give someone a sprig of lily of the valley, and they'll have happiness and good fortune for the whole year. ♥

★ DANGEROUS BEAUTY ★
    ☠️  CAUTION!  ☠️

Don't let the sweet appearance fool you! Lily of the valley is HIGHLY TOXIC. Every part contains cardiac glycosides that can cause serious harm.

★ PERFUME POWERHOUSE ★
    ___
   /   \~~~
  |  o  |
   \___/
   Fancy!

The flower has one of the most beloved scents in the perfume world! Its sweet, fresh aroma is nearly impossible to extract naturally, so perfumers usually recreate it synthetically. Dior, Chanel, and Guerlain have all made famous muguet perfumes. 

★ LANGUAGE OF FLOWERS ★

In Victorian flower language (floriography), lily of the valley means "you've made my life complete" and symbolizes the return of happiness, sweetness, and humility. It's also associated with motherhood!

This delicate-looking flower is actually super hardy! It spreads through underground rhizomes (root stems) and can take over a shady garden where other plants struggle. It's a Zone 2-7 plant, meaning it can survive some seriously cold winters!

Each stem produces 5-15 tiny bell-shaped flowers. They bloom in late spring and are usually white, though there's a rare pink variety called "Rosea." The flowers only last 3-4 weeks!
